Cosmic Chronicles


  • Classes: 9th-12th
  • Description:The event is for story writing and will consist of two rounds - the first offline; the second online. Students who will be selected for the second round will be notified a day before the final round. The prompt for round one is mentioned above. The prompt for the second round will be revealed on the spot, after which the participants will be given 2 hours to write their story.
  • Rules:
  •  Word limit for the first round 1 is 1500 words (minimum 700 words). The entry will be
  •  submitted via google forms in either a .pdf file or .docx file via Google Forms.
  •  The link for the form will be shared post registration.
  •  The word limit for round 2 is 1000 words, the prompt for which will be shared in the online round.
  •  The students will have 2 hours to write and submit their stories in a .pdf file or .docx file via
  •  Google Forms
  • Judgement Criteria :
  •  Originality & Creativity
  •  Plot
  •  Grammar
  •  Theme and consistency
