    Welcome To TechXplore 2021

    Ryan Ghaziabad is proud to invite you to our 2021 TechXplore event, a place for students across various schools to come together and participate and enrich themselves. Here you will participate in various events for students of classes from 6th to 12th grade, a social gathering of talented student and retain knowledge and wonderful memories.

    Check out our event schedule!

    • 2nd February, 2022

      Opening ceremony

      The opening ceremony of the 4-day technology and innovation fair to be attended by all the participants.Timings: 9:00am to 10:30am

    • 2nd February, 2022

      Celestial Arcade

      The event will be conducted over the course of 3 days (2nd to 4th February). The event will start at 11:00am on day 1 and at 10:00 am for the next two days.

    • 2nd February, 2022

      Crisis Cabinet

      The event will take place on 2nd February, 2022 and will consist of 2 rounds. The first round will be 1 hour long and will start at 11:00am. After a 40-minute interval, the second round will begin at 12:40pm.

    • 2nd February,2022

      Interstellar Insight

      The participants will sit in the meeting of the final round of the group discussion from 12:40pm onwards on 2nd February. After the round is over, they'll be given 1.5 hours to submit their pieces.
