    Ryan Ghaziabad is proud to invite you to our 2021 TechXplore event, a place for students across various schools to come together and participate and enrich themselves, perform in one of the various events for students of classes from 6th to 12th grade, a social gathering of talented student, to participate

    Expand your Horizons

    Our varied and diverse events provide you a host of opportunities to challenge yourself and create something new - and to be part of a community of other students such as yourself. Open for all schools and all ages, there is nothing quite like this as an opportunity to grow and improve and strengthen your skills
